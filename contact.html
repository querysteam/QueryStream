Common Questions

How quickly can you set up our chatbot?

Most chatbots are live within 3-5 working days. We handle everything from development to integration, so you don't need any technical knowledge.

Do you work with small businesses?

Absolutely! We specialize in UK small businesses. Our solutions are designed to be affordable and effective for local shops, salons, restaurants, and service providers.

What happens during the free consultation?

We'll discuss your current customer service challenges, understand your business needs, and show you exactly how a chatbot could help. No sales pressure, just honest advice.

Can the chatbot handle complex queries?

Our AI is trained specifically for your business and can handle most common queries. For complex issues, it seamlessly transfers to your team with full context.

Is there a long-term contract?

No contracts required. You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We believe in earning your business through great service, not locking you in.

Will it work with our existing website?

Yes! Our chatbots integrate with any website platform including WordPress, Shopify, Squarespace, and custom-built sites.
