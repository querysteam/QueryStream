Our Story

QueryStream was born from a simple observation: while tech giants talk about AI revolution, local UK businesses were still drowning in repetitive customer queries. Hair salons spending hours on the phone explaining prices. Restaurants fielding the same menu questions dozens of times a day. Fitness centres explaining membership options over and over.

We realized that AI chatbots didn't need to be complex enterprise solutions costing thousands. They needed to be simple, effective, and built specifically for the businesses that form the backbone of the UK economy — the corner shops, local restaurants, independent salons, and family-run services that make our communities thrive.

That's why we created QueryStream: AI that actually works for real businesses, with real budgets, serving real customers.
