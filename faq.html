Technical Details

How does the chatbot learn about my business?

We train your chatbot using multiple sources:

  • Consultation interview: We ask detailed questions about your business, services, pricing, and common customer queries
  • Website content: We analyze your existing website for key information
  • FAQ documentation: Any existing FAQs, brochures, or informational materials
  • Customer interaction history: Common questions you receive via phone, email, or in-person

The AI is then trained to understand your business context, brand voice, and how to respond appropriately to customer queries. It learns your specific terminology, pricing structure, and policies.

Will the chatbot work on my existing website?

Yes! QueryStream integrates with virtually any website platform:

  • Popular platforms: WordPress, Squarespace, Wix, Shopify
  • Custom websites: HTML, React, any modern web technology
  • E-commerce platforms: WooCommerce, Magento, BigCommerce
  • Booking systems: Calendly, Acuity, custom booking tools

We add a small code snippet to your website (we can do this for you), and the chatbot appears as a professional chat widget. It matches your website's design and brand colors.

If you don't have a website yet, we can recommend simple, affordable solutions.

What happens when the chatbot can't answer a question?

When the chatbot encounters a question it can't answer confidently, it:

  • Acknowledges the limitation: "That's a great question, but I want to make sure you get accurate information."
  • Offers alternatives: Provides your phone number, email, or contact form
  • Captures the enquiry: Collects the customer's contact details and question for you to follow up
  • Suggests related topics: Offers information on similar subjects it can help with

We also monitor these "unknown" questions and regularly update your chatbot to handle them in future, continuously improving its performance.
